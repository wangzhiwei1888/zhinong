How did AgriSharable start?
The goal of AgriShaiable is to empower importers with
intelligent tools and insights to make better decisions
and be more profitable.
We realized how hard was for importers to make decision of registration and guiding farmers with better crop solution. Without Data and expert, the importers business all depends on the exporters and the environment. Most time, they import what the competitor's import products. It means big competition,less profit and feww help for farmers.
For these reason, we decided to create an Artificial Intelligence decision engine for crop protection, The “AIgro”, to support the importer's sales judge the worse pest and give real-time and workable solution to farmers, and also get the data for analysis and forecast.
